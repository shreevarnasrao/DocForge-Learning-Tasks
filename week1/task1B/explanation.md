## Machine Learning Concepts
 
 
### 1) What is Machine Learning?
 
Teaching a computer to find patterns in data and make predictions.give it thousands of examples and it figures out the rules itself.
 
### 2) What is Supervised Learning?
You give the model both the questions and the answers during training.First give data to the ones and both the question and answer during training then after training ask it to predict to  the data that it hasnt seen
 
### 3)What is Unsupervised Learning?
 
You give the model only the questions,no answers.It not been told what to look for and what to do and no answers.No right or wrong answer. Just pattern discovery.
No supervision 

 
### 4)Key Terms
 
#### Features
The inputs.The X's.The data you feed in to make a prediction.like gender,lunch,ethicity, etc
 
#### Target Variable
The output..The Y. What you're trying to predict.The one you want to make a guess.Ex is total score.
 
#### Training Data
The data the model learns from.In this example 800 rows out of 1000
